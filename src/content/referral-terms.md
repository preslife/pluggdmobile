**Share-to-Earn & Referral Program Terms**

Last updated: August 18, 2025

## Program Overview

Pluggd's Share-to-Earn & Referral Program ("Program") allows users to earn credits by referring new users and sharing content. These terms supplement our main Terms of Service.

## Eligibility

- Must be a registered Pluggd user in good standing
- One referral account per person/email address
- Cannot refer yourself or create fake accounts

## How It Works

### Referral Rewards
- **Friend Signs Up**: Both you and your friend receive 200 Credits (£2)
- **First Purchase**: You receive 1,000 Credits (£10) when your friend makes their first purchase of £5 or more
- **Subscription**: Both you and your friend receive 2,000 Credits (£20) when they start a subscription

### Share-to-Earn
- Earn 200 Credits (£2) when someone signs up through your shared link
- Link must be shared through the official sharing tools
- Signup must occur within 7 days of link creation

## Credit Terms

- Credits have no cash value and cannot be exchanged for money
- Credits expire 12 months after earning
- Credits can be used for purchases, subscriptions, and other platform services
- Credits cannot be transferred between accounts

## Program Rules

### Prohibited Activities
- Using bots, scripts, or automated tools
- Creating fake accounts or emails
- Manipulating the referral system
- Spamming or unsolicited sharing
- Self-referrals or circular referrals

### Fair Use
- Maximum 50 successful referrals per month
- Share links expire after 30 days
- Pluggd reserves the right to audit referral activity

## Modifications and Termination

- Pluggd may modify, suspend, or terminate the Program at any time
- Changes will be communicated via email and website updates
- Earned credits remain valid according to their original terms

## Violations

Violations may result in:
- Forfeiture of earned credits
- Account suspension or termination
- Removal from the Program

## Disputes

All disputes related to the Program are subject to the dispute resolution process outlined in our main Terms of Service.

Questions about the Share-to-Earn & Referral Program should be directed to support@pluggd.fm.
