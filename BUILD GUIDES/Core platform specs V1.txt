Detailed Design Specifications: Advanced Fan Engagement & Community Building SaaS for ArtistsCore Objectives• Build a creator-first platform for music artists, producers, and their fans.• Enable direct fan support, collaborations, education, and monetization.• Combine marketplace, community, and live experiences into one seamless hub.1. Artist Monetization Tools• Music & Beats Storefrontso Upload, price, and sell releases, beats, and sample packs.o Licensing templates for beats (exclusive/non-exclusive).• Crowdfunding Campaignso Artist-led fan funding with tiered rewards.• Fan Subscriptionso Monthly membership tiers with exclusive perks.• Merch Storeo Sell branded physical and digital merchandise.• Tipping & Giftingo Direct tips, badges, and virtual gifts from fans.• NFTs / Collectibleso Tokenized releases, exclusive drops, and collectibles.• Analytics Dashboardo Sales, streams, fan engagement, and payouts tracking.2. Community Hub• Contests & Challengeso Branded competitions with prizes.• Collaborations Hubo Post briefs, find collabs, split payments.• Community Radioo Shared queue of tracks from members.• Forums & Threadso Feedback, wins, general discussion.• Daily Promptso Creative challenges with tags.• Quests & Gamificationo XP, streaks, achievements, and leaderboards.• Badges & Recognitiono Milestones, contest wins, community contributions.• Trending Topicso Surface hottest tags and themes.3. Live & Events• Live Streaming & Sessionso Performances, studio breakdowns, Q&As.• Virtual Meet & Greetso 1:1 or group fan video chats.• Ticketing & Event Managemento Free/paid events with seat limits.• Masterclasses & Courseso Structured educational content.• Calendar Integrationo Centralized view of upcoming events.4. Fan Engagement• Exclusive Fan Clubso Private groups per artist.• Leaderboardso Top fans by engagement, tips, or XP.• Interactive Giftingo Virtual stickers, shoutouts, fan spotlights.• Credits Systemo Platform-wide currency for purchases, tips, and unlocks.• Recommendation Engine (AI)o Personalized music & collab suggestions.5. Creator Growth• Mentorshipo Book 1:1 time with experienced creators.• Placements & Synco Opportunities for film, TV, ads.• Brand Partnershipso Artist campaigns with sponsors.• Customizable Storefrontso Artist branding, layout, and featured content.
